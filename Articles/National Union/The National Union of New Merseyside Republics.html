The National Union, officialy the National Union of New Merseyside Republics (Spanish: Unión de las Repúblicas de Nuevo Merseyside) is a parliamentary commonwealth consisting of the union of the two Republics via the Parliament of the National Union. It sits at the west mouth of the New Mersey River and its surrounding area.The Union covers an approximate of 359,154.98 km2 and has an approximate population of 8.300.000. Its capital and biggest city is Hydrantville ( metro pop. 410.000, city pop. 280.000)
